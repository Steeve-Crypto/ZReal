# ZReal Case Study Notes

This file is conceptual product planning, not a list of implemented customer outcomes.

Potential use cases:

- private issuer workspace for real estate tokenization preparation
- legal document extraction before tokenization
- audit trail for ZSA issuance attempts
- investor dashboard for recorded holdings

These scenarios require additional production work before real capital is involved:

- verified ZSA issuance backend
- investor purchase flow
- distribution model
- KYC/AML and securities compliance
- legal review for each jurisdiction
